ABOUT US

Discover Our Story/Learn About Our Vision and Values
C4 Protection is a premier security service provider located in Houston, Texas. Our team of licensed and professional security guards is dedicated to providing top-notch security services for nightlife venues. We understand the importance of maintaining a lively atmosphere while managing large crowds of people and ensuring the safety of customers.
At C4 Protection, we take security seriously and prioritize the safety of your business, employees, and property. Our licensed security guards are highly trained and knowledgeable, and we ensure that they are up-to-date with the latest security measures and protocols.
Our security guards are equipped to handle a variety of situations, including celebrity protection and crowd control. They are responsible for screening customers at entrances, monitoring identification checks, patrolling the property of the venue, and ensuring parking lots are clear of unauthorized vehicles and pedestrians.
We understand that working as a security guard in a nightlife venue can be physically demanding, which is why we only hire candidates who are capable of lifting heavy objects and standing for extended periods. Our security guards report directly to our Security Team Leader, who oversees all security operations and ensures that all security measures are in place and followed.
At C4 Protection, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional security services to our clients. Our team of licensed and professional security guards is dedicated to keeping your nightlife venue safe and secure. Contact us today to learn more about our security services.
